Her Quiet Radiance

It’s in her hazel-brown eyes—

a gentle blend of earth and forest,

warm and deep, turning green only when kissed by light,

holding both calm and quiet wonder.


Her lips, soft and unspoken poetry,

curl into a quiet smile that feels familiar,

like a secret only she knows,

gentle, playful, and effortlessly real.


Dark brown waves of hair frame her face,

falling freely like a midnight breeze,

each strand catching the light,

a perfect balance of wild and calm.


But it’s her soul that truly shines—

kindness woven into every word,

a heart that listens, gives, and heals,

caring deeply in a world that often forgets.


Her beauty is more than what you see—

it’s in how she makes you feel.

Her beauty is more than what you see—it’s in how she makes you feel.
~ A Quiet Muse ~
